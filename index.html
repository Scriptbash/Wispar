Wispar

Stay up-to-date with academic journals and the latest research articles!

Download now!

Wispar (Wisdom + Scholar) is an Android and iOS app designed to make academic research simpler, faster, and less overwhelming. Follow the journals and topics you care about, get notified when new articles appear, and read them offline in a clean, distraction-free interface.

Custom Feeds

Follow journals and keywords to never miss relevant research.

Send to Zotero

Link your Zotero account and send publications directly.

Unpaywall & EZproxy

Get open-access papers or bypass paywalls via EZproxy.

Abstract Translations

AI translations for abstracts in your preferred language.

Offline Reading

Save articles and PDFs for offline use anywhere.

Private & Open Source

No tracking, no ads, your data stays local.
